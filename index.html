Dual-Credit Classes

While attending Steeleville High School I took several SWIC classes through the dual-credit program. Essentially, I gained both high school and college credits while taking the classes, which were typically at a higher level than their high school counterparts.

One of my favorite dual-credit classes was Information Processing, one of the only computer classes we had in our small town school. There, we learned the basics of several programs in Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, Access, and Powerpoint. While not part of the SWIC class, we also had a web development club until COVID hit in 2020. We didn't get very far, but I created my first webpages in that club.

Genealogist

I developed an interest in my family history at a young age after listening to some of my grandparents' stories. I built my first family tree in 2015, but began doing my own research in the summer of 2018. After greatly expanding my family tree in my first two years of research, I began putting more effort into verifying my connections and gathering sources. These days, I spend most of my genealogy time connecting sources to profiles and adding links to project trees.
